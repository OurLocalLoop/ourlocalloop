Our Local Loop is a Western Australian business discovery platform connecting people with the talented local vendors, creatives and service providers right in their own backyard.

Why Our Local Loop exists

Finding a great local business shouldn't be hard. But between scrolling endless Facebook groups, trawling through Google results, and asking around in community pages, it often is.

Our Local Loop was created to change that. One place to find local makeup artists, photographers, florists, cake designers, hair stylists and more, all based in your community, all ready to help make your next event special.

We started in Perth, Mandurah and Rockingham because that's home. As the community grows, so will we.

What we believe

🌱
Local first
Every business on Our Local Loop is based right here in Western Australia. No national chains, no faceless brands.
🤝
Community driven
We exist to connect people, not replace the personal touch. Real businesses, real people, real conversations.
✨
Quality over quantity
Every listing is manually reviewed. We'd rather have fewer great businesses than a directory full of noise.
💜
Built with care
Our Local Loop is an independent platform made with love for the businesses and communities it serves.

For businesses

Our Local Loop is designed to help small businesses get discovered by the right people at the right time. Whether you're a solo makeup artist, a home baker, or an established event florist, if you're local and you're great at what you do, we want you here.

Listings are currently by application only. We review each business personally before adding them to the directory. To apply, fill in our listing form or get in touch directly.
